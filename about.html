About Our Webpage

ShetiMitta is a comprehensive agricultural marketplace designed to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers. Our platform provides a seamless experience for buying and selling fresh produce, while also offering valuable tools for farm management including diary tracking and AI-powered assistance.

Analytics

Track sales performance with interactive charts and insights

Vision & Goal

Our Vision

To revolutionize Indian agriculture by creating a technology-driven ecosystem that empowers farmers, ensures fair prices for their produce, and provides consumers with access to fresh, quality agricultural products. We envision a future where every farmer has digital access to markets, knowledge, and resources.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to increase farmer incomes by eliminating middlemen and connecting them directly with consumers. We aim to: provide a user-friendly digital platform, offer AI-powered farming guidance, enable product listing and sales management, and build a sustainable agricultural community.
